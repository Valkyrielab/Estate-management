Fees and Charges

2.1. All fees for professional time spent are charged at an hourly rate and depend on the person involved and the complexity of the work required.
2.2. All fees exclude Value Added Tax (“VAT”) at the applicable VAT rate from time to time (currently 15%).
2.3. Disbursements (e.g. traveling expenses, courier costs, government charges, third party professional fees, banking, legal, accounting and audit fees, telephone, facsimile and postal expenses) will be charged separately and in addition to Legacy’s fees.
2.4. A surcharge of 25% - 50% of the fees may be charged for urgent matters and after-hours attendances.
2.5. Legacy receives up to a maximum of 1.0% (one percent) on the interest received on amounts held and administered by Legacy.
